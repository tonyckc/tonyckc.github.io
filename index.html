Biography

I am a PhD student in Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, co-supervised by Prof. Yan Hong and Dr. Li Haoliang.
Before that, I obtained my Master Degree with Computer Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, my advisor was Prof. Xiaorong Pu.
I'm currently working on :
(1) Transfer Learning and its Applications in Medical Images
(2) Medical Image Processing, including CT reconstruction, detection and so on
(3) Digital Signal Processing, including TEM signal and EEG signal denoising
Reviewers:
